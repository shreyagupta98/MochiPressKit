JEVCS Presents:

Mochi Ga Daisuki

Visual novel for all the Davis mochi lovers

About

Mochi Ga Daisuki is a fun and light-hearted visual novel experience inspired by the daily anxieties faced by college students in Davis. The story revolves around you, Ushi, a young and energetic (and slightly vulgur) UC Davis student determined to complete a task assigned by your CS professor, Kuma, in order to achieve the full level of happiness = a life's worth of mochi.

Stay safe on the road, make decisions based on your persoality, and find the quickest and safest route to the mochi festival BEFORE time runs out!

Ushi: Well... what the f*** are you waiting for? Go play the game!

Features

- Multiple-branch story-telling
- Three dynamic, relatable characters encountered by students on a daily basis
- English text (waiting for translations into Japanese, Spanish, and Hindi)
- Unique, original assets - Engaging soundtrack
